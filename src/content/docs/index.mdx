## About

**ExamManager V3** is the open-source T3/Drizzle rewrite of ExamManager — classroom timers, multi-exam sessions, timetables, and seating plans for schools and invigilators.

This deployment is a **deprecated portfolio showcase**. The product is no longer actively sold; signup and billing are disabled.

## Product focus

- Classroom exam timer for low-distraction countdown displays
- Multi-exam sessions when rooms and cohorts overlap
- Timetable and seating-plan tools for exam weeks
- School-scale planning with result insights on higher tiers

## Pricing (illustrative)

Plans match the V1 showcase:

- **Standard** — $0/mo
- **Basic** — $9.99/mo (most popular)
- **Professional** — $19.99/mo
